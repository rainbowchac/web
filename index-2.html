Welcome To Rainbow Learning Academy!

We know that choosing a child care center is one of the most difficult decisions a parent has to make.

Many child care centers do not provide the nurturing, qualified teaching staff, variety of activities, technology and educational advantages that you want for your children. Our preschool programs are based upon the principles of developmentally appropriate practice.


Our days are filled with enriching activities, exciting learning experiences, and wholesome meals and snacks. Our primary goal is to prepare your children for a life-long love of learning, and to instill self-confidence and self-esteem. Before you make your child care decision, please accept our invitation to visit.

Parents are their children's first teachers; therefore we honor your role as a parent and as the first and most important teacher in your child's life. We will support this relationship by providing educational opportunities that help each child reach important developmental milestones. Through nurturing and individualized attention, we will take great care to provide a safe, secure, and fun learning environment for your child.
