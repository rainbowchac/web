Our goals

Happy Time fully embraces the following goals:
To prepare children for kindergarten and beyond.
To lay a strong foundation of skills necessary for future learning.
To teach the children about the awareness of their surroundings, caring about their environment, and obey the rules instilled in them by elders.
To develop enthusiasm for learning by providing multiple opportunities for learning.
To develop an awareness and the importance of being part of the community.

 

Rainbow Learning Academy working to provide those childhood opportunities and experiences that further promote the total development of children and allows them to be “CHILDREN.” Our goal is to give each child a warm environment that encourages the development of socialization skills, independence, and a positive self-image. We helping children become aware of feelings and opinions of others, practicing safety habits and develop a feeling of trust between children and teachers
